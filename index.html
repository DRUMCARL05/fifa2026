How to Play

Scoring System

  • 4 pts — Exact scoreline (draw) AND correct penalty winner
  • 3 pts — Exact scoreline
  • 2 pts — Exact draw score but wrong penalty winner, OR wrong draw score but correct penalty winner
  • 1 pt — Correct team advances, wrong scoreline
  • 0 pts — Complete miss

Round Multipliers

  • Round of 16 — ×1
  • Quarter-Finals — ×2
  • Semi-Finals — ×3
  • 3rd Place — ×1
  • Final — ×4

Champion Bonus

Predict the tournament winner for a flat +15 bonus points. Locks when the Round of 16 begins.

Your Predictions

Picks save instantly to the cloud as you tap. Each match locks automatically at kickoff. If you predict a draw, a penalty winner selector slides in.
